Bills Tables Project

Inspiration

A personal project using excel which aims at ensuring each housemate equally contributes to utilities throughout the tenancy. To solve this issue one account was used to as a pot where each housemate pays into the pot. The tables track the amount paid into the pot for each housemate and the monthly expenses of utilities for a shared house of 5 people in university housing. The tables also predicted the monthly excess or deficit expenditure of each housemate by using the quotations from 3 utility companies. A yearly total is predicted which can be used for budgeting purposes in the future.
