Integrated Design Project

Inspiration

A coursework module which aimed at developing design skills and creativity though an interdisciplinary engineering team project that covers aspects of realistic product design. The main aim was to address global themes, the chosen them was climate adaptation systems. The decided to focus on food security so a modified hydroponic greenhouse which grows 3 crops was designed. I was tasked with generating a design solution to mass plant seeds. A modified robotic seeder was designed to automate the process of mass planting within the greenhouse. Using 2 different sowing methods from agriculture and germination rates of each crop, a modified fluted wheel device was a designed to measure and control the seeds from a hopper before dispensing them into the hydroponic substrate.
